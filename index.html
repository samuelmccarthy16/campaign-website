Biographies

Madam Annie A. Jones

Madam Jones, a native of Weabo District in Maryland County, Liberia, West Africa, embodies resilience and community dedication. Forced to migrate to the U.S. during Liberia's civil war, she transformed her struggles into empowering families and single mothers navigating the complexities of raising children in a society that often prioritizes separating families over supporting them.

Settling in Charlotte, NC, she became a pillar in the community, recognized for her efforts to foster positive change. Her initiatives focus on youth empowerment, education, and humanitarian support.

Musu Raynes

Musu Raynes, a dedicated wife, mother, and real estate professional, is passionate about expanding access to affordable housing and empowering communities. She actively participates in various community organizations and lives by the motto: "Walk the walk."
